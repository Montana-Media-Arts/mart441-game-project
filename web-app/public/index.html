About the Game

This is a game that was created by the class of MART 441. The goal was to provide a fun interactive fighting game involving simple controls. Over the course of the of development the game has been challenged by obstacles and roads but has been honed to perfecting, we of the development team wish that you will as well appreciate the effort put into this project.


The knights of the realm have been called forth to fight for their kingdom. You have been given the honor of defending your king in a display of might and skill.
