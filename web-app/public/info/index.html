GAME INSTRUCTIONS

  • Press the "PLAY NOW" button to enter the game

  • To change gravity, relative to the position of your character, use the arrow keys

    • Use the W A S D keys to move left and right, or up and down depending on which way your gravity is switched at the time. The direction that faces up relative to your character will be the jump key, while perpinducilar keys will be to walk side to side.

  • To attack your opponent use the Spacebar key
